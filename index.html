Seong Joon Oh

I am a research scientist at NAVER AI Lab.

I did my PhD in computer vision and machine learning at Max-Planck Institute for Informatics, under the supervision of Bernt Schiele and Mario Fritz. I received the Master of Mathematics and Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics at University of Cambridge.
